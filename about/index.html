Hi there! You are about to begin reading my webpage. Relax. Concentrate. Dispel every other thought. Let the world around you fade. Best to close the door; the TV is always on in the next room. 1

My research focuses on designing and implementing algorithms to simulate physics and chemistry for meaningful, real-world applications. These efforts are unified by a drive to push the boundaries of state-of-the-art methods for modeling many-body quantum processes in molecules and materials.

In chemistry, I’ve recently focused on covalent inhibition mechanisms in the context of drug discovery for myotonic dystrophy. On the physics side, I continue to explore techniques to understand the effects of strong electronic correlations in transition metal oxides, with potential applications in future quantum and energy technologies.

I'm a passionate hill walker, staunch advocate of the slow food movement, and always keen to learn something new.
